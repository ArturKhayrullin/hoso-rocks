About Brand
BUSCEMI is an American luxury footwear and accessories brand for men, women and children sold globally in recognized luxury retailers and specialty boutiques worldwide. Founded in 2013 by Jon Buscemi. He committed to his craft following the idea of "The Most Elegant Sneaker" — a union of his obsession with the most creative street style and the highest quality and craftsmanship in the world.
